Frequently Asked Questions

You'll find answers to common inquiries regarding Creative Tim's platform and pricing plans.

What is the All Access Pass?

The All Access Pass from Creative Tim provides access to Creative Tim UI (shadcn/ui Blocks ready for AI Models), Creative Tim Club (130+ UI Kits, Admin, and Mobile Templates), David UI PRO, Material Tailwind v3 PRO, Creative AI Pixels, AI Agent Galichat, n8n Workflow Templates, and "Roots of UI/UX Design" book.

With this package, you pay one price for a multitude of powerful tools.

What's included in the Pro AI Platform?

The Pro AI Platform includes everything in Starter, plus Creative AI Pixels, AI Agent Galichat, 200+ n8n automation templates, and AI-powered workflows. This is the complete package for teams building with AI.

Can I use the platform for both personal and business projects?

Yes! The tools included are perfect for personal, freelance, or business projects. They will help streamline your workflows and accelerate development.

What is Creative AI Pixels?

Creative AI Pixels is our AI-powered visual generation tool that helps you create illustrations, marketing assets, and custom branding materials with ease.

What's included in the Starter Platform?

The Starter Platform includes Creative Tim UI (shadcn/ui Blocks), Material Tailwind PRO, David UI PRO, Creative Tim Club (130+ Templates), and Roots of UI/UX Design book. Perfect for developers who want to build production-ready UIs.

How do I access the tools after purchasing?

Once you complete your purchase, you will gain immediate access to your chosen plan and can start using the tools directly from your account dashboard.

What is Material Tailwind?

Material Tailwind is a component library built with Tailwind CSS and React, designed to help developers and designers set up their web projects faster and more easily. It has over 4 million installs.

What is AI Agent Galichat?

AI Agent Galichat allows you to deploy intelligent AI agents for customer support, sales qualification, and internal knowledge management. You can export or host these agents.
